About Helena

Helena, the capital city of Montana, is a hidden gem in the heart of the Treasure State. Rich in history and surrounded by natural beauty, Helena offers a unique blend of urban amenities and outdoor adventures.

Key Information

  • Population (2020): 32,091
  • Region: Western Montana
  • Median Household Income: $61,324
  • Incorporated: October 30, 1864

History and Culture

Helena was founded during the Montana gold rush and has since evolved into a vibrant capital city. Its historic downtown, with its Victorian architecture, tells the story of its rich past.

Attractions

  1. Montana State Capitol
  2. Gates of the Mountains Wilderness Area
  3. Cathedral of Saint Helena
  4. Montana Historical Society Museum

Did You Know?

Helena is home to the "Million Dollar Mile," a stretch of road that once housed the most millionaires per capita during the gold rush era.
