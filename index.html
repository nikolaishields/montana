About Montana

Montana, known as "The Treasure State," is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, rich history, and diverse landscapes. From the rugged Rocky Mountains to the vast plains of the east, Montana offers something for everyone.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Helena
  • Largest City: Billings
  • Population: Approximately 1.1 million (as of 2021)
  • Nickname: Big Sky Country
